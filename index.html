Gheymeh (Persian Yellow Split Pea Stew)

Gheymeh is a traditional Persian stew made with yellow split peas, tomato, dried limes, and tender chicken or beef. It is commonly served with rice and topped with crispy fried potato strips. This dish is deeply rooted in Iranian culture and is often prepared for family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or beef, cut into small pieces
  • Yellow split peas
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Tomato paste
  • Dried limes (limoo amani)
  • Turmeric
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté the chopped onion until soft and golden. Add turmeric and stir briefly to release its aroma.
  2. Add the chicken or beef and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for a few minutes until it darkens slightly and blends with the meat.
  4. Add the rinsed split peas, dried limes, salt, and pepper. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients.
  5. Reduce heat, cover the pot, and allow the stew to simmer until the split peas are soft and the flavors are fully developed.
  6. Serve hot over rice and top with fried potato strips if desired.
